Asees Full Punjabi Movie 2018

"Asees" is a Punjabi language movie that was released in 2018. It was directed by Rana Ranbir and starred Rana Ranbir himself, along with Neha Pawar, Kuljinder Singh Sidhu, Rupinder Rupi, Sardar Sohi, and others. The movie is a family drama that revolves around the relationship between a mother and her son. It is set in a rural village in Punjab and explores the themes of love, sacrifice, and forgiveness. The story follows the journey of a young man named Kuldeep (played by Rana Ranbir), who is estranged from his mother after an argument. He later realizes the value of his mother's love and sacrifices and seeks to reconcile with her. The movie was well-received by audiences and critics and was praised for its emotional depth and strong performances.
